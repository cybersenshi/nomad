Audio

Every event is unique and will have its own set of audio challenges. Whether you require flyable speaker systems, on-stage monitor systems, feeds for media and broadcast, or clear audio for your CEO's address -- we have the experience and inventory to cover all of your audio needs and can design a system to meet the specifics of your event.

  • State-of-the-art equipment by Digidesign, Yamaha, Midas, EAW, Klarkteknik, and others
  • Line Array / Flyable speaker systems
  • Distributed speaker systems
  • Digital and Analog Consoles
  • Wireless, hard-wired, podium, band, and shotgun mic systems
  • Monitor systems
  • A FULL staff of audio engineers who can't wait to mix your next event

Lighting

Whether lighting is used for setting moods or to showcase an important part of your event, it is one element of an event that people always remember. We carry a wide range of lighting and rigging products including truss systems, chain motors, conventional and moving lights, dimmer rack systems, uplighting and pinspots. Our lighting team will help you design the look you are after. We also make custom gobos (your organization's logo etched in metal or glass) to project on walls or set pieces.

  • Moving light fixtures
  • Source four fixtures
  • PAR lighting
  • Genie towers
  • Trussing
  • Pipe & Base
  • Pinspots
  • DJ lights / mirror balls
  • Lighting controllers
